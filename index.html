City Weather Comparisons

Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purpose of this project was to analyze how weather changes are you get closer to the equator. To do this analysis, a random sampling of weather condition data from at least 500 cities from around the world was taken using the OpenWeatherMap API. From that dataset, four weather conditions--maximum temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed--were then compared with the cities' latitudes through Matplotlib scatter plots to determine if they can predict what the weather will be like.


This site provides the source data extracted, the visualizations created, and the findings of the analysis.
